Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the cafeteria? +
The cafeteria is on the 1st floor of the Recreation Building, next to the track field.
How to use library Wi-Fi? +
Select "Campus_Student", enter your student ID as the username and your DOB as the password.
Lost and Found? +
Please visit the Student Affairs Office in the Main Admin Building.
